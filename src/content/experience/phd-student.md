During my Ph.D. in Statistical Sciences at the University of Padua (supervisors: Prof. Giovanna Capizzi and Prof. Peihua Qiu), I focused on advancing **online outlier detection** and **stochastic optimization** methods for statistical process monitoring.

My research resulted in publications in top-tier journals including the *Journal of Quality Technology*, *Statistics & Computing*, *Quality Engineering*, and the *Journal of Statistical Software*. I also developed the **StatisticalProcessMonitoring.jl** package, a comprehensive Julia framework for real-time process monitoring.

Additionally, I applied my expertise in real-world contexts through consulting projects on sequential monitoring systems for structural health monitoring, involving algorithm development in R and C++.

**Thesis**: *Advanced Statistical Process Monitoring using Simulation-Based Algorithms* (2024)

**Research stay**: University of Florida (Jan 2023 - Dec 2023), supervised by Prof. Peihua Qiu
