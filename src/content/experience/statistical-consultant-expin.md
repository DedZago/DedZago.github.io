Developed a sequential monitoring system in **R** and **C++** for anomaly detection in vibrating string strain gauges, addressing critical challenges in structural health monitoring of civil infrastructure.

Designed a novel algorithm for optimal alarm threshold selection using **stochastic optimization** techniques, enabling adaptive and efficient detection of structural anomalies while minimizing false alarms.

The project combined statistical process control, computational efficiency (C++ implementation), and modern optimization theory to deliver a production-ready monitoring solution.
